Price Industries

    View Details
  • From bar grilles to linear diffusers, air distribution products come in a variety of forms to meet various performance and visual requirements. Price is the largest manufacturer of air distribution products in the US and has extensive research, development and testing capabilities.
  • Chilled beams are an excellent way to downsize your air delivery system for spaces with high sensible and low latent loads. Active chilled beams use a primary airstream to induce room air across cold and hot water coils to condition the space and get the necessary air change rate, whereas passive beams use convection for air movement. By utilizing water as a thermal transfer fluid, chilled beams are more efficient than a standard mixed air system and are an excellent solution for hospitals and other applications that do not allow recirculated air.
  • Underfloor air delivers conditioned air from below the floor level as opposed to a traditional overhead ceiling approach. In a fully mixing system, underfloor air is specifically designed to condition and mix only the occupied zone. As such, underfloor air distribution is capable of managing higher loads and can offer savings over a traditional overhead mixing system. Additionally, it is a great solution for dynamic and frequently changing zone spaces.
  • Displacement ventilation involves delivering conditioned air at a low velocity and higher temperature to the occupied zone with minimal induction and mixing. Heat loads are conditioned through heat plumes and extracted at the ceiling. Some advantages of displacement ventilation include significant energy savings, improved thermal comfort and indoor air quality.
