  • Thermafuser VAV Diffusers
  • Thermafuser diffusers are ceiling diffusers with built-in room thermostats and self adjusting dampers for varying the air volume. Dampers within the diffuser continuously adjust to vary the volume of air flow (heating and cooling) into the room to control space temperature and maintain consistent throw patterns, leading to increased comfort and productivity. Thermafusers are a great solution for temperature problem zones.

  • Punkah and Nozzle Diffusers
  • Punkah and nozzle diffusers are excellent solutions for spot cooling or high throw applications such as areas with large open spaces or high ceilings.   • Fabric Ductwork
  • Fabric duct is a flexible and cost effective air distribution alternative to traditional metal duct. Fabric duct is lighter than traditional metal duct and is not subject to rusting, making it perfect for corrosive environments such as indoor pools and coastal locations. It is also a great option for open ceiling applications such as retail and warehouses.
